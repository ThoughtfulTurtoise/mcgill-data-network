About us

Making data accessible at McGill since 2015.

Founded in 2015 under the Management Undergraduate Society at the Desautels Faculty of Management, McGill Data Network has grown into one of McGill's leading student communities for data, analytics, AI, and technology-driven careers.

Our mission is simple: make data accessible to everyone and bridge the gap between students and professional opportunity.

Through panels, workshops, hackathons, competitions, and networking events, MDN helps students build practical skills, meet industry professionals, and discover where data can take them.

What we do

Four ways we connect students with data and industry.

From technical workshops and AI hackathons to panels and networking events, MDN creates spaces where students learn by doing and connect with the people who shape their careers.

Industry Panels

We bring professionals from consulting, finance, technology, and AI into the room with McGill students, unpacking real career paths and how data drives decisions.

Featuring speakers from EY, BNP Paribas, KPMG & RBC.

Technical Workshops

Practical sessions built around real skills, not theory. Students work hands-on with Python, SQL, Tableau, Alteryx, and AI workflows.

Live deliverables, not passive slides.

Hackathons & Competitions

Hands-on events where students solve real problems under time pressure, collaborate in teams, and present to judges, companies, and peers.

Flagship: the inaugural AI Hackathon.

Networking & Career Events

Networking cocktails, office visits, and recruitment events that connect students directly with alumni, recruiters, founders, and industry leaders.

Including our 10th Anniversary Cocktail.
